The Blue Jay Guest House is open all year.

A Victorian town house constructed in 1881 we offer a central location with views over Oban bay and the island of Mull.

Violet and Jack

Violet and Jack hope guests will enjoy the peace, stunning scenery, abundant wildlife, our beautiful wild flowers and ancient heritage including duns and castles. We are happy to advise you on local places of interest and to help you really obtain the greatest benefit from your stay. Read our guide.
